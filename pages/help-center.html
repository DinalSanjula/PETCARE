Popular Questions

What are credits for?

Everyone is encouraged to get 10 free credits by introducing 3 pet lovers to sign up and use PetCare. Credits can be used to get discounts on future bookings.

How to be a pet sitter or walker?

To be a pet service provider, it is free. You need to complete the following steps: 1) Sign up on PetCare, 2) Complete your profile with photos and bio, 3) List your services and set your prices, 4) Get approved by our team, 5) Start accepting bookings!

Cancellation by Pet Owner
